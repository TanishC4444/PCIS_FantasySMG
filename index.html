What is Trading?

Trading is the act of buying and selling assets, such as stocks, to generate profit. Understanding trading helps develop financial literacy and strategic thinking.

Why is Trading Important?

Trading drives liquidity, price discovery, and wealth creation. Learning about trading equips individuals with the tools to navigate financial markets.

How Hedge Works

Hedge simulates real-world trading using fake money for a risk-free learning experience.

  • Data Collection: Uses yfinance for live data.
  • Backend: Powered by Flask for processing trades.
  • Tracking: Local storage for portfolio management.

Key Features

  • Live Data: Real-time stock prices.
  • Portfolio Management: Track performance seamlessly.
  • Simulated Trading: Risk-free investing experience.
  • Educational Resources: Learn trading strategies effectively.
